Lasagna

Description

A plate of lasagna that you can make to!

This beautiful dish is not only delicious, but also authentic! By following the following steps, you can have it for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 1 Table spoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 pound Beef
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon pepper
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese, and any other cheeses of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 400 degrees
  2. cook onion on medium high for 4 minutes
  3. In small bowl stir your cheeses of choice
  4. cook lasagna noodles
  5. mix onion with beef and cook for another 4 minutes
  6. mix in marinara sauce with the beef and onion, cook for 10 minutes
  7. Layer all ingredients into casserole
  8. Bake lasagna after assembling for 25 minutes
